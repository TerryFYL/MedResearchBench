# Figure Descriptions: Pankratz et al. (2022) Cancer Control

## Figure 1: 1-Year CRC Cause-Specific Survival Trends by Stage and Race/Ethnicity
- Type: Multi-panel line plot
- Content: Temporal trends in 1-year survival probability (y-axis) by year of diagnosis (x-axis, 1992-2018), with separate panels for localized, regional, and distant stages. Five lines per panel representing NHW, NHB, AIAN, Hispanic, and API groups.
- Key pattern: Distant-stage panel shows improving trends for all groups with AIAN and NHB on lower but converging trajectories.

## Figure 2: 5-Year CRC Cause-Specific Survival Trends by Stage and Race/Ethnicity
- Type: Multi-panel line plot
- Content: Same structure as Figure 1 but for 5-year survival. More pronounced racial/ethnic disparities visible compared to 1-year survival.
- Key pattern: Greater separation between racial/ethnic groups in 5-year vs. 1-year survival, especially for regional and distant stages.
