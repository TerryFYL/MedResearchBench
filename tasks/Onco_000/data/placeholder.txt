# Data Source: SEER Program (Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results)

This task uses data from the NCI SEER Program, 12 registries (1992-2018).

## How to Access

1. **Submit a data use agreement**: https://seer.cancer.gov/data/access.html
2. **Request SEER Research Data** (requires signed DUA and approved account)
3. **Download using SEER*Stat software**: https://seer.cancer.gov/seerstat/

## Required Data
- **Database**: Incidence - SEER Research Data, 12 Registries, Nov 2020 Sub (1992-2018)
- **Case selection**: Primary site = Colon and Rectum (C18.0-C20.9), Histology = Adenocarcinoma (8140-8389)
- **Variables needed**: Age, Sex, Race/Ethnicity, Year of Diagnosis, SEER Historic Stage, Survival Months, Vital Status, Cause of Death, SEER Cause-Specific Death Classification

## SEER Registries (12)
Alaska, Connecticut, Atlanta, Rural Georgia, San Francisco-Oakland, San Jose-Monterey, Hawaii, Iowa, Los Angeles, New Mexico, Seattle-Puget Sound, Utah

## Population Coverage
- Approximately 14% of the US population
- Over 399,000 CRC cases in the study period (1992-2018)

## Data Format
- SEER*Stat can export data as ASCII text or CSV
- Data dictionary and variable documentation available at: https://seer.cancer.gov/data-software/documentation/

## Access Requirements
- Free access after signing DUA
- Individual-level data; protected health information removed
- Processing time: typically 1-3 business days for account approval
