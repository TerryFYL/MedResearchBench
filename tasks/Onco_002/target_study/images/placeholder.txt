# Figure Descriptions: Liang et al. (2020) Clinical and Translational Gastroenterology

## Table 1: Patient Characteristics by Time Period
- Type: Descriptive table
- Content: Demographics, tumor characteristics, staging distribution, treatment patterns, and area-level income for CRC patients across the four time periods (1991-1997, 1998-2001, 2001-2005, 2006-2010).

## Table 2: Multinomial Logistic Regression for CRC Stage at Diagnosis
- Type: Results table
- Content: Adjusted odds ratios (95% CI) for distant-stage diagnosis by race/ethnicity, area-level income quartile, and rurality across four time periods. Shows Black patients 24-39% more likely to present with distant disease.

## Table 3: Cox Proportional Hazards Regression for CRC-Specific Mortality
- Type: Results table
- Content: Adjusted hazard ratios (95% CI) for CRC-specific death by race/ethnicity, income, and rurality across four time periods. Shows Black patients with 16-21% increased mortality vs. White patients.
