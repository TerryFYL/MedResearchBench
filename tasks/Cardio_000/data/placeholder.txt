# Data Source: NHANES 2017-2018

This task uses publicly available data from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) 2017-2018 cycle.

## Required Data Files

Download from: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/nchs/nhanes/continuousnhanes/default.aspx?BeginYear=2017

1. **Demographics (DEMO_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/DEMO_J.XPT
2. **Dietary Interview - Day 1 Total Nutrients (DR1TOT_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/DR1TOT_J.XPT
3. **Blood Pressure (BPX_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BPX_J.XPT
4. **Blood Pressure & Cholesterol Questionnaire (BPQ_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BPQ_J.XPT
5. **Physical Activity (PAQ_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/PAQ_J.XPT
6. **Smoking (SMQ_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/SMQ_J.XPT
7. **Alcohol Use (ALQ_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/ALQ_J.XPT
8. **Body Measures (BMX_J.XPT)**: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BMX_J.XPT

## Data Format
- Files are in SAS Transport format (.XPT)
- Can be read with R (foreign::read.xport or haven::read_xpt) or Python (pandas.read_sas)
- Merge files on SEQN (respondent sequence number)

## Analytic Sample
- Adults aged >= 20 years
- Complete dietary recall (DR1DRSTZ = 1)
- Complete blood pressure measurements
- Non-pregnant females
- Final analytic sample: approximately 5,569 participants (after exclusions)

## Access
- No registration or approval required
- Data freely downloadable from CDC/NCHS website
