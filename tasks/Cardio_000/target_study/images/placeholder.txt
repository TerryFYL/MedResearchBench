# Figure Descriptions — Cardio_000

## Figure 1: Restricted Cubic Spline (RCS) Dose-Response Curves
- Panel A: Sodium intake (mg/day) vs. adjusted OR for hypertension
  - X-axis: Dietary sodium intake (mg/day)
  - Y-axis: Adjusted odds ratio (95% CI) for hypertension
  - RCS with 4 knots showing relatively flat relationship with wide CIs
  - Reference line at OR = 1.0
  - P for nonlinearity displayed
- Panel B: Potassium intake (mg/day) vs. adjusted OR for hypertension
  - Similar format to Panel A

## Figure 2: Subgroup Analysis Forest Plot
- Subgroups: Sex (Male/Female), Age (20-39, 40-59, 60+), BMI (<25, 25-30, 30+)
- Point estimates with 95% CI for sodium-hypertension association within each subgroup
- P for interaction values shown
- All confidence intervals crossing the null
