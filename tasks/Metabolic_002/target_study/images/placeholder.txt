# Figure Descriptions: Zhu et al. (2022)

## Figure 1: Study Flow Diagram
Participant selection flowchart showing NHANES III population → exclusion of non-examined → exclusion of non-euthyroid → exclusion of non-diabetic → final analytic sample of 1,830 euthyroid adults with diabetes.

## Figure 2: RCS Dose-Response Curves
Restricted cubic spline plots showing the continuous nonlinear (U-shaped) association between serum TSH (x-axis, mIU/L) and mortality hazard ratio (y-axis). Panel A: All-cause mortality; Panel B: Cardiovascular mortality. Both panels show the reference line at HR = 1.0, with 95% confidence interval shading. The nadir of the U-curve is in the medium-normal TSH range (~1.3-2.1 mIU/L), with both lower and higher TSH values associated with elevated HRs. P for nonlinearity < 0.05 indicated for both endpoints.

## Figure 3 (if applicable): Subgroup Forest Plots
Forest plots showing TSH tertile hazard ratios stratified by age (<60 vs ≥60 years) and sex (male vs female), with test for interaction P-values.
