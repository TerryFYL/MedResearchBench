# Figure Descriptions — Metabolic_003

## Figure 1: NAFLD Prevalence by Demographic Subgroups
- Bar chart or forest plot showing NAFLD prevalence (with 95% CIs) across subgroups:
  - Age groups (20-44, 45-64, ≥65)
  - Sex (Male, Female)
  - Race/ethnicity (Mexican American, Other Hispanic, NH White, NH Black, NH Asian)
  - BMI categories (Normal, Overweight, Obese I, Obese II-III)
  - Diabetes status (Yes, No)
- Overall prevalence reference line at 26.7%

## Figure 2: Advanced Fibrosis Prevalence Among NAFLD-Positive Individuals
- Dual bar chart comparing NFS-based (18.1%) and FIB-4-based (3.7%) fibrosis estimates
- Stratified by key subgroups (age, sex, BMI, diabetes)
- Illustrates the discordance between noninvasive fibrosis scoring systems

## Figure 3: NAFLD Prevalence Trend Across NHANES Cycles
- Line chart showing NAFLD prevalence across 2011-12, 2013-14, 2015-16, 2017-18
- Error bars for 95% confidence intervals
- P-for-trend annotation
