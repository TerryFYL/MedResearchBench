# Data Source: NHANES 2005-2008 and 2015-2018 (4 Cycles)

This task uses publicly available data from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) spanning four cycles: 2005-2006, 2007-2008, 2015-2016, and 2017-2018.

## Important Note on Cycle Selection

NHANES cycles 2009-2014 did NOT include sleep apnea questionnaire items in the Sleep Disorder module (SLQ). Therefore, this study pools the 2005-2008 cycles (which included sleep apnea questions) with the 2015-2018 cycles (which re-introduced sleep apnea questions).

### Cycle 2007-2008 (suffix _E)
Replace _D with _E and 2005 with 2007 in URLs above.

### Cycle 2015-2016 (suffix _I)
Replace _D with _I and 2005 with 2015 in URLs above.

### Cycle 2017-2018 (suffix _J)
Replace _D with _J and 2005 with 2017 in URLs above.

## Data Format
- Files are in SAS Transport format (.XPT)
- Can be read with R (haven::read_xpt) or Python (pandas.read_sas)
- Merge files on SEQN (respondent sequence number) within each cycle
- Pool cycles by appending rows and adjusting survey weights

## Analytic Sample
- Adults aged ≥20 years
- Complete sleep disorder questionnaire data (SLQ050, SLQ060)
- Complete fasting subsample laboratory data (glucose, triglycerides, HDL, insulin)
- Complete body measures (BMI)
- Exclusion: pregnant women, missing key covariates

## Multi-Cycle Weight Adjustment
- When pooling 4 NHANES cycles, divide 2-year weights by 4
- Adjusted fasting weight = WTSAF2YR / 4
- Adjusted MEC weight = WTMEC2YR / 4

## Access
- No registration or approval required
- Data freely downloadable from CDC/NCHS website
