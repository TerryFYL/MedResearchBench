# Data Source: NHANES 2013-2018 (Pooled 3 Cycles)

This task uses publicly available data from three NHANES cycles: 2013-2014, 2015-2016, and 2017-2018.

## Required Data Files (per cycle)

### NHANES 2013-2014
- Demographics: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/DEMO_H.XPT
- Blood Pressure Exam: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/BPX_H.XPT
- BP Questionnaire: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/BPQ_H.XPT
- Medical Conditions: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/MCQ_H.XPT
- Diabetes: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/DIQ_H.XPT
- Body Measures: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/BMX_H.XPT
- Rx Medications: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2013-2014/RXQ_RX_H.XPT

### NHANES 2015-2016
- Demographics: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/DEMO_I.XPT
- Blood Pressure Exam: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/BPX_I.XPT
- BP Questionnaire: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/BPQ_I.XPT
- Medical Conditions: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/MCQ_I.XPT
- Diabetes: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/DIQ_I.XPT
- Body Measures: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/BMX_I.XPT
- Rx Medications: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2015-2016/RXQ_RX_I.XPT

### NHANES 2017-2018
- Demographics: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/DEMO_J.XPT
- Blood Pressure Exam: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BPX_J.XPT
- BP Questionnaire: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BPQ_J.XPT
- Medical Conditions: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/MCQ_J.XPT
- Diabetes: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/DIQ_J.XPT
- Body Measures: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/BMX_J.XPT
- Rx Medications: https://wwwn.cdc.gov/Nchs/Nhanes/2017-2018/RXQ_RX_J.XPT

## Data Format
- Files are in SAS Transport format (.XPT)
- Merge within each cycle on SEQN, then append across cycles
- When pooling 3 cycles: divide 2-year MEC weight (WTMEC2YR) by 3

## Analytic Sample
- Non-pregnant US adults aged >= 18 years
- Complete blood pressure measurements
- Final pooled analytic sample: approximately 16,531 participants
