# Figure Descriptions — Cardio_003

## Figure 1: Hypertension Control Cascade by Race/Ethnicity
- Stacked/grouped bar chart showing the cascade: Prevalence -> Awareness -> Treatment -> Control
- Separate bars for each racial/ethnic group (White, Black, Hispanic, Asian, Other)
- Y-axis: Percentage (0-100%)
- Clear visual showing the "drop-off" at each cascade stage by race/ethnicity
- Black adults: highest prevalence bar, steepest drop from treatment to control
- Asian adults: lowest control bar despite moderate prevalence

## Figure 2: Forest Plot of Adjusted Odds Ratios for BP Control
- Reference group: NH White
- Comparators: NH Black, Hispanic, NH Asian, Other
- Point estimates with 95% CI horizontal lines
- Vertical reference line at OR = 1.0
- All minority groups showing OR < 1.0 (lower odds of BP control)
